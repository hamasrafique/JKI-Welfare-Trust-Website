Our Projects

Explore our diverse initiatives across various fields aimed at uplifting communities.

Food Drives

At JKI Welfare Trust, we organize regular food drives to ensure that no one goes to bed hungry. Our food drives distribute nutritious meals to those in need across various regions, targeting underprivileged communities and disaster-affected areas.

Ration Distribution

Our ration distribution programs provide essential supplies to families in need, particularly during times of crisis. These packs include food staples, hygiene products, and other necessities, ensuring sustenance for those struggling to make ends meet.

Education

Education is the key to a better future. We focus on providing free educational resources, scholarships, and support to underprivileged children. Our goal is to empower communities by equipping the next generation with the tools they need to succeed.

Health

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right. We conduct health camps, provide medical assistance, and work on spreading health awareness in marginalized areas. Our efforts aim to improve the overall health and well-being of the communities we serve.
